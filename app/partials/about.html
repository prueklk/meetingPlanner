What is Meeting Agenda builder?


Meeting Agenda is a platform to create and plan your meetings in the easiest possible way. You can add an activity of one of the four types-Coffee Break, Group work, Discussion and Presentation and also set the duration of each activity. You can create a meeting plan and then move your activities to the plan. You can see how you manage your time from the activity color bar.
