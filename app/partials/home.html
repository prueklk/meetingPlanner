What is Agenda?



Agenda is a platform to create and plan your meetings in the easiest possible way. You can add an activity of one of the four types-Coffee Break, Group work, Discussion and Presentation and also set the duration of each activity. You can create a meeting plan and then move your activities to the plan by simple drag and drop. You can see how you manage your time from the activity color bar.

In addition, there is weather forecast information provided on the certain day and time.Information about the start time, end time and total time can also be viewed.
